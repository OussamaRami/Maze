About

When I was a kid, I lets say 7 years old year 2007, I remember the time when my father bought a DVD player for playing movies. I still remember when he used to take a CD from the envelope and insert it into the DVD player. What I didn't know, and what came as a surprise, was that one of the CDs he bought contained a video game, more precisely a collection of games. Initially, I was hooked, playing the whole day and inviting my friends to play together. Whether it was Super Mario or a 2D shooter, it was fun. So, I started liking games from then on. In the couple of years after that 3 years to be exact, It was the time when I was introduced to a PC for the first time,I played alot of games and of course I wanted to make a game, but I didn't know how or where to start, and I was very frustrated. But now, I have an opportunity to make that childhood dream come true, so I decided to choose this project.

This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School. You can find it on GitHub.
